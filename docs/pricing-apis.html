Pricing

Consumer pays the owner's price. Owner keeps 95%. Per-call or subscription.

Pricing model

The owner sets a price per call. The consumer pays exactly that amount. From that payment, 5% is deducted and goes to the platform. The owner receives the remaining 95%.

consumer pays = owner's price
owner receives = owner's price × 95%
platform fee   = owner's price × 5%

Example breakdown

Owner's PriceConsumer PaysOwner ReceivesPlatform
$0.01$0.01$0.0095$0.0005
$0.10$0.10$0.095$0.005
$1.00$1.00$0.95$0.05

Setting your price (owners)

When you register your API, you set the price per call in USDC. This is visible to consumers in the catalog. You can update your price at any time from the web console.

Subscriptions

Owners can also offer monthly subscription plans for unlimited access. The same 95/5 split applies to subscription payments.

No hidden fees. The consumer pays the owner's stated price. The 5% platform fee is deducted from the owner's side. Plus a small network transaction fee.
