How It Works

One Simple Rule

Consumer pays the owner's price. 5% is deducted from the owner.

Owners set their own pricing — per request, per stream minute, per task, per query, or flat monthly. The consumer pays exactly that amount with Visa, Mastercard, or USDC — no crypto wallet required. From that payment, 5% goes to the platform and 95% goes to the owner.
