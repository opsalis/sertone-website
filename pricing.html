How It Works

One Simple Rule

Consumer pays the owner's price. 5% is deducted from the owner.

The owner sets their price per call (for example, $0.01, $0.10, or $5.00). The consumer pays exactly that amount with Visa, Mastercard, or USDC — no crypto wallet required. From that payment, 5% goes to the platform and 95% goes to the owner.

Owners can also offer monthly subscriptions for unlimited access. Same split: 95% to owner, 5% to platform.
