Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What We Collect

Sertone collects your email address only when you sign up for updates, solely to send you product announcements.

We store no other personal data. No IP addresses, no location data, no browsing information, no cookies.

2. How We Use It

Your email is used exclusively to send you product updates and announcements. We will never share, sell, or transfer your email to any third party.

3. Retention

You can request deletion of your email at any time. Once you unsubscribe, your email is permanently deleted from our systems.

4. Unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link included in any email we send you.

5. Platform Architecture

The Sertone platform itself operates on a zero personal data architecture. We never collect names, addresses, phone numbers, or any identifying information. The platform interacts exclusively with public wallet addresses.

6. Contact

For questions, contact us at privacy@sertone.net.
